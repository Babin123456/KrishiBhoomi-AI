# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At KrishiBhoomi AI, we value the privacy of our farmers and administrators.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect soil parameters, farm sizes, crop leaf photos uploaded for disease detection, and multilingual voice logs to improve AI prediction accuracy.

## 2. Satellite & GIS Data
Field coordinates (lat/lng) are used solely to query Copernicus Sentinel-2 satellite data and local Mandi location listings.

## 3. Data Protection
Your information is stored securely in encrypted databases and is never sold to third-party advertisers.
